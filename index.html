About Me

Welcome to my site! My name is Holden Melander, and I am a small business owner based in Lakewood, Washington.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Business Management from Brigham Young University with an Emphasis in Enterpernual Management. I have had the opportunity to work for various companies in the retail field, including Walmart, Staples, and 20/20 events in Utah. I have owned a franchise with 20 employees for the past two years.

My business skills include team leadership, sales strategy, inventory management, and customer service. I am passionate about driving sales by building strong relations with my clientele and the local community.

Outside work, I enjoy reading good fantasy novels, playing obscure board games, and spending time with my family.
